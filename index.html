The Zoo Animal Logger

By Mechasparrow, a.k.a Michael Navazhylau

Problem

Information is recorded and submitted mostly on paper forms.

This produces a bottleneck in information gathering and management for the Animal Registry and Zoo Keepers.

  • Zoo keepers have to note information about animals on Paper forms

  • They then send this to the animal registry for it to be saved
  • This means that information regarded the animal and its upkeep can not be known until the paper forms are recieved

  • This is a bottleneck in regards to time and the information can be misplaced or misrecorded along the way.
Opportunity

This problem provides an opportunity for optimization of animal monitoring and submission process.

  • Method to limit the possible error in recording animal behavior and feedings

  • Faster way to send submissions to the registry
Solution

We propose a solution to this problem in the form of a staff-facing digital logging solution.

This allows the Zoo Keeper or Staff person to log and submit animal data.

Future
  • Integrate it with the animal registry database/backend
  • Add a consumer facing element for visitors/animal adopters to obtain certain information about their favorite animal
  • Create data visualizations for animal logs
  • Expand it to work with other zoo animals
